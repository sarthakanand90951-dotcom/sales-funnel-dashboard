Critical Drop-Off Identified
The Qualification → Proposal transition shows a ~37% lead drop — the single largest leak in the funnel, responsible for 20–25% of revenue leakage. Immediate intervention needed.
✅
Actionable Recommendation
Implement structured lead scoring and qualification criteria. Reducing drop-off at this stage by just 10% could add ~150 qualified opportunities to the pipeline monthly.

Optimisation Potential
Proposal → Negotiation stage holds the second-highest drop. Better proposal templates and faster turnaround times can improve this by 6–8%, directly impacting closed-won rate.
